# Leaf Link - UXperience 2025 (3rd Place Winner)

**Role:** Lead UX Designer | **Tools:** Figma, Miro, Google Workspace  
**Tracks:** General & CBRE

---

## Project Overview
Leaf Link is a UX design initiative to create an **active, engaging, and user-friendly digital platform for modern workplaces**. Its goal is to increase employee performance and improve overall workplace wellbeing by combining productivity tools with emotional wellness support. Inspired by nature, the platform features a calming visual identity, soft greens, rounded edges, and minimalist icons that promote focus and peace.

The centerpiece of the experience is **Leafly**, an empathetic AI chatbot that helps users track moods, manage stress, and build healthy habits. Leaf Link encourages collaboration, connection, and engagement across in-person, remote, and hybrid work environments.

---

## Objectives
- Improve employee wellbeing, engagement, and productivity.
- Create an accessible and inclusive web platform for all devices.
- Blend task management with emotional wellness support.
- Foster team connection through shared wellness challenges and insights.

---

## Process
1. **Research:** Conducted empathy mapping, user interviews, and surveys.
2. **Information Architecture:** Designed site maps and structured content hierarchy.
3. **Ideation:** Developed user personas, user stories, and journey maps.
4. **Design:**  
   - Created moodboards and style guides.  
   - Developed low- and high-fidelity wireframes in Figma.  
   - Iterated designs based on mentor feedback to ensure cohesion.  
5. **Testing & Feedback:** Ran design sprints and usability testing to refine interaction, accessibility, and visual consistency.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **Interactive Dashboard:** Task bar, calendar, and reminders to support mindful productivity.  
- **Leafly AI Companion:** Provides mood check-ins, wellness tips, gentle break reminders, and encourages positive routines.  
- **Team Dashboard:** Shared wellness events, motivational insights, team productivity tracking, and lighthearted engagement challenges.  
- **Mood Tracker:** Employees can log emotions, personal notes, and photos to reflect and share growth over time.
